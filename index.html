About Me

I was born and raised in Madrid, Spain. My family and I moved to the United States when I was 8 years old due to the global financial crisis. I was lucky enough to have a beautiful, hard-working mother that paved the way for me to be successful in life. After graduating high school, I had to move away from my family for a second time and move into the dorms at the University at Buffalo. I completed my first year as a computer engineering major. However, I was not happy with the updated curriculum I would've faced my second-year. I then decided to switch career paths and pursue a degree in Business Administration. I thought that by setting my concentrations to both International Business and Management Information Systems, I would get the most out of my undergraduate career. Thankfully, I wasn't wrong and enjoyed every class I took those last 3 years. Taking so many different types of classes, I found other areas of business that I liked, such as accounting and statistics. Having now graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration, I do not regret a single thing. I am continuing my studies at UB for one more year in order to receive my Master of Science in Management Information Systems diploma by May 2023.
